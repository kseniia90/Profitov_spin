Сookie

Cookies are small files consisting of letters and numbers. A cookie file may be sent to your computer's browser or other device each time you visit certain websites. The purpose of cookies is to store basic information, such as visitor preferences. Cookies will be sent back to the originating website upon each subsequent visit to that site. Cookies are useful as they allow websites to recognize your browser or device over a period of time.

How we use cookies?

We use them to make the https://profitov.partners website easier to use. Cookies can be used to speed up your future activities on our site. We also use cookies to compile anonymous information - aggregate statistical data that allows us to understand how people use our site to help us improve its structure and content. We cannot identify you personally based on this information.

What types of cookies do we use?

On the https://profitov.partners website, we use two types of cookies - "session cookies" and "persistent cookies". Session cookies are temporary files that remain on your device until you leave the site. Persistent cookies remain on your device for a longer period or until you manually delete them (how long a cookie remains on your device will depend on the duration or "lifetime" of the specific file and your browser's settings).

How to disable and/or delete cookies?

Most browsers accept cookies by default. You have the right to accept or decline the transmission of cookies to your computer or other device at any time by changing the settings in your browser according to your preferences regarding cookies. You can control and/or delete cookies at your discretion. To learn more about this, please visit https://aboutcookies.org. You can delete all cookies already on your computer or other device and configure most browsers to refuse cookies. However, in this case, you will need to manually adjust some settings each time you visit a site. As a result, some services and features may not work properly. To opt out of tracking by the Google Analytics service on all websites, visit https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.
